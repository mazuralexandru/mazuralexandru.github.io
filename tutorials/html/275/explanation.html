275. Padding Fundamentals: Purpose

Files Modified: None

We now move to the next layer of the box model: Padding.

  • Purpose: Padding creates space between the element's content area and its border. It's like adding empty space around the text within a box before drawing the box's outline.
  • Visual Effect: It increases the visible size of the element because the background color/image extends into the padding area.
  • Spacing: It pushes the content away from the border.

Padding is crucial for preventing text or other content from bumping right up against the element's edges or border, improving readability and visual appeal.
