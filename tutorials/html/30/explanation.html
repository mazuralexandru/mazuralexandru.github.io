30. Anatomy Recap: Element = Tag + Content + Tag

Files Modified: None

Let's quickly recap the relationship between tags and elements for standard, non-void elements:

Opening Tag + Content + Closing Tag = Element

Example:

<p>This is the content of the paragraph element.</p>
  • <p> is the opening tag.
  • This is the content of the paragraph element. is the content.
  • </p> is the closing tag.
  • The entire line constitutes the paragraph element.

Understanding this distinction (tag vs. element) is key to discussing HTML accurately.
