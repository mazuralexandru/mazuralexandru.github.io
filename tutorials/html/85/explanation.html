85. Preparing for Strikethrough: Removing <sub>/<sup> Examples

We've covered subscript (<sub>) and superscript (<sup>) and their importance for semantic accuracy and accessibility.

Now, to focus on the final inline semantic element in this section, <s> (strikethrough), we will remove the <sub> and <sup> examples.

Why Remove Code?

  • Focus: Isolate the concept and appearance of the strikethrough element.
  • Relevance: The sub/superscript examples are unrelated to the semantic meaning of strikethrough.
  • Clean Slate: Provide simple paragraphs as a baseline for demonstrating <s>.

Code Change:

We have removed the paragraphs containing the <sub> and <sup> examples from index.html and adjusted the surrounding text.
