453. background-clip

Files Modified: style.css

While background-origin determines where positioning starts, background-clip determines how far the background (color or image) actually extends or paints.

  • Purpose: To control the painting area of the background.
  • Syntax: background-clip: value;
  • Values:
    • border-box (Default): The background extends to the outer edge of the border. It paints underneath the border (if the border is transparent or semi-transparent, like dashed/dotted).
    • padding-box: The background extends to the outer edge of the padding (the inner edge of the border). The background is clipped at the border.
    • content-box: The background extends only to the edge of the content box. It does not paint under the padding or border.
    • text (Experimental/Prefixed): Clips the background to the shape of the foreground text. This is used for effects like image-filled or gradient-filled text, usually requiring the text color to be transparent.

}
