344. list-style-position: Visual Difference

Files Modified: None

Let's recap the visual difference between list-style-position: outside and inside:

  • outside (Default):

    • Marker sits in the padding/margin area.
    • Wrapped text aligns under the start of the text, creating a hanging indent effect.
    • Generally preferred for standard lists for better readability.

  • inside:

    • Marker is part of the text flow, at the beginning of the first line.
    • Wrapped text aligns under the marker.
    • Can look slightly cramped or less organized for multi-line items compared to outside.
