636. Reset & Reason 2: Representing Symbols (Copyright, Trademarks)

We've thoroughly covered escaping reserved characters. Now let's look at the second major reason for using entities: representing symbols that might not be on a standard keyboard or where using an entity ensures consistency.

Resetting the Page:

To focus on symbol entities, we will remove the previous examples related to escaping <, >, &, ", and '.

Reason 2 Recap: Convenience/Compatibility for Symbols

  • Entities provide a standard way to include common symbols.
  • While direct UTF-8 input is often possible and preferred, entities can be useful for clarity or specific workflows.

  2. Updated the <h1> and paragraph text to focus on symbols.
