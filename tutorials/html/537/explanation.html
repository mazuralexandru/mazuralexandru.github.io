537. Introduction to Module 11: Multimedia and Embedding

Welcome to Module 11! We've explored text, lists, links, images, metadata, global attributes, tables, forms, and semantic page structure. This module will equip you with the knowledge to embed video and audio content effectively and accessibly, understand different media formats, provide text tracks like captions, and recognize when to use specific embedding elements.
