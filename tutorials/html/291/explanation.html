Next, we will revisit the lang attribute, which we initially introduced on the <html> element (Lesson 18), and explore its use on specific inline or block elements to denote language changes within a document. We will also introduce its companion attribute, dir.

To prepare, let's remove the title and <abbr> examples.

Why Remove Code?
