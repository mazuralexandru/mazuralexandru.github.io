455. background-clip: Values

Files Modified: None

Let's review the effect of the main background-clip values:

  • border-box (Default): The background (color or image) extends to the outside edge of the border. It is painted underneath the border itself.
  • padding-box: The background extends to the outside edge of the padding (the inside edge of the border). The background is clipped by the border area; it does not paint underneath the border.
  • content-box: The background extends only to the edge of the content area. It does not paint under the padding or the border.

The text value is more specialized and clips the background to the shape of the text itself, often used with transparent text color to create text filled with images or gradients.
