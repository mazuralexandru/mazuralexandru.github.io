298. Longhand Margin Properties: margin-right

Files Modified: style.css

Next is margin-right.

  • Purpose: Sets the amount of margin space specifically on the right edge of the element, outside its right border.
  • Syntax: margin-right: value;
  • Values: Same as margin-top (lengths, percentages relative to container width, auto, negative values allowed).

✅ Observe style.css:

  • We've added margin-right: 50px; to the .box rule.
.box {
  width: auto; 
  height: auto; 
  min-width: 200px; 
  max-width: 400px; 
  min-height: 150px; 
  max-height: 200px; 
  overflow: auto; 
  background-color: cornflowerblue; 
  padding: 20px 5px 15px 30px; 
  margin-top: 2em; 
  margin-right: 50px; /* Added specific right margin */
  border-bottom: 4px solid red; 
  border-radius: 15px; 
}

✨ Observe the Preview: The blue box should now have 50px of clear space to its right, pushing any subsequent elements (if they were inline-block or floated) away from its right edge. Since it's a block element taking up available width (up to its max-width), this might not be visually obvious unless the container is narrow or other elements are positioned nearby.
