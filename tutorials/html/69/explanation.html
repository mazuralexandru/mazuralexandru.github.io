69. <title>: Importance

Files Modified: None

Don't underestimate the importance of a well-crafted <title> element. It plays a key role in:

  1. Usability: Helps users understand what page they are on and quickly find it among multiple tabs or in their history/bookmarks. A generic title like "Home Page" is much less helpful than "CompanyName - Innovative Solutions for X".
  2. Accessibility: It's often the first piece of information announced by screen readers, providing essential orientation for visually impaired users. A clear, descriptive title is crucial for navigation.
  3. Search Engine Optimization (SEO): Search engines place significant weight on the <title> tag. Including relevant keywords helps search engines understand the page's topic and rank it appropriately. It's also the text users see in search results, influencing their decision to click.

Always take the time to write a meaningful, unique title for every page of your website.
