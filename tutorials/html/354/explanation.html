354. ::marker: Browser Support

Files Modified: None

The ::marker pseudo-element is a relatively modern addition to CSS.

  • Support: It enjoys good support across all major modern browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge).
  • Older Browsers: It will not work in older browsers like Internet Explorer.
  • Fallback: If ::marker styles are not supported or fail to apply, the browser will fall back to the styles defined by the list-style-* properties.

For projects needing to support very old browsers, you might need to rely on older techniques (like background images or ::before pseudo-elements) for complex marker styling. However, for most modern web development, ::marker is a viable and often cleaner approach for customizing marker appearance.
