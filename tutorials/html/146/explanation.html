146. Anchor Element (<a>): Purpose and Role

What is the main job of the <a> element?

Purpose & Role:

The anchor element's primary purpose is to create hyperlinks, enabling navigation and connection between resources on the web. Specifically, it allows users to:

  1. Navigate between HTML documents: Jump from one web page to another (the most common use).
  2. Navigate within a document: Jump to a specific section or element on the same page (using fragment identifiers, #).
  3. Link to other resources: Provide access to files (like PDFs, images, zip archives), trigger email clients (mailto:), or initiate phone calls (tel:).

Essentially, <a> tags transform parts of your content (text, images) into interactive elements that bridge your document to other locations or actions. They are the fundamental mechanism that makes the web interconnected.
