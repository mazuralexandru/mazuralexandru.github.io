Next, we return to elements that provide structure and semantic grouping within a form: <fieldset> and its associated caption element, <legend>. These are particularly important for accessibility when grouping related controls like radio buttons or address fields.

To focus on these structural elements, we will remove the hidden input examples and other content from our form.
