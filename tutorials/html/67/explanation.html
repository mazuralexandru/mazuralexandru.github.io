67. <title>: Content Rules

Files Modified: None

The content of the <title> element should be plain text only.

  • Allowed: <title>About Us - Company Name</title>
  • Not Recommended/Ignored: <title>About <strong>Us</strong></title> - The <strong> tags will likely be ignored or displayed literally as part of the title text, not rendered as bold.

Keep the title text descriptive and concise. It should accurately reflect the content of the specific page.
