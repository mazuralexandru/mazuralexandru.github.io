439. background-position: Syntax

Files Modified: None

Let's review the syntax options for background-position:

  • One Value:
    • background-position: center; (Equivalent to center center)
    • background-position: top; (Equivalent to center top)
    • background-position: left; (Equivalent to left center)
    • background-position: 25%; (Equivalent to 25% 50% or 25% center)
    • background-position: 50px; (Equivalent to 50px 50% or 50px center)
  • Two Values: (Horizontal Vertical)
    • background-position: top left; or left top; (Order of keywords doesn't matter if using two keywords)
    • background-position: center bottom;
    • background-position: 20px 40px; (20px from left, 40px from top)
    • background-position: right 10%; (Align right edge, 10% from top)
    • background-position: 10% top; (10% from left, align top edge)
  • Three/Four Values (Edge Offsets): Allows specifying an offset from a specific edge other than top/left.
    • background-position: bottom 10px right 20px; (20px from right edge, 10px from bottom edge)
    • background-position: right 50px center; (50px from right edge, centered vertically)

The two-value syntax is the most common. Percentages are useful for positioning relative to the container size, while keywords and lengths provide absolute positioning.
