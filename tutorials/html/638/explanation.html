638. Representing Symbols: Other Common Examples

Let's add a few more common examples of useful named entities for various symbols.

Common Technical & Punctuation Entities:

  • &sect; (§): Section sign.
  • &para; (¶): Paragraph sign (Pilcrow).
  • &deg; (°): Degree symbol.
  • &plusmn; (±): Plus-or-minus sign.
  • &times; (×): Multiplication sign (visually distinct from the letter 'x').
  • &divide; (÷): Division sign.
