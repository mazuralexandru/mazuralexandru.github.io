550. Reset: Preparing for Other Video Attributes (crossorigin)

We have covered the main attributes related to video source (src), display (controls, width, height, poster), and playback (autoplay, muted, loop, preload, playsinline).

Next, we'll briefly touch upon the crossorigin attribute, which relates to fetching media from different domains and interacting with it via JavaScript.

To focus on crossorigin, we will reset the <video> element again, removing the src and playsinline attributes added in the previous steps.

Why Reset/Adjust?

  • Focus: Isolate the crossorigin attribute and its purpose related to CORS.
  • Clarity: Demonstrate the attribute without the context of specific playback modes.

Code Change:

  1. Removed the src and playsinline attributes from the <video> tag in index.html.
  2. Updated the <h1> and paragraph text.
