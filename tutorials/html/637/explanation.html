637. Representing Symbols: Currency (&euro;, &pound;, &yen;)

Continuing with common symbols, let's look at currency signs.

Currency Symbols:

  • While many currency symbols can be typed directly (especially $), using entities for others can ensure consistency or handle cases where direct input is awkward.
  • &euro; (€): Euro sign.
  • &pound; (£): Pound Sterling sign.
  • &yen; (¥): Japanese Yen / Chinese Yuan sign.
