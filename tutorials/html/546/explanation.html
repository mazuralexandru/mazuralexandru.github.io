546. Reset: Preparing for Playback Attributes

We've established the basic <video> structure and added attributes for source (src), controls (controls), dimensions (width, height), and placeholder image (poster).

Now, let's focus on attributes that control the playback behavior: autoplay, muted, loop, and preload.

To isolate these attributes, we will temporarily remove the src and poster attributes from our <video> element. We'll keep controls, width, and height for visual context.

Why Reset/Adjust?

  • Focus: Concentrate purely on the attributes controlling how and when the video plays or loads, without the distraction of specific source files or posters.
  • Clarity: Demonstrate each playback attribute individually or in common combinations.

Code Change:

  1. Removed the src and poster attributes from the <video> tag in index.html.
  2. Updated the <h1> and paragraph text.
